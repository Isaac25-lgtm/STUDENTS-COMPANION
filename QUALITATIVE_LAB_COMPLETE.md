## üéØ Methodologies Supported

### 1. **Thematic Analysis** (Braun & Clarke)
**6 Phases:**
1. Familiarization with data
2. Generating initial codes
3. Searching for themes
4. Reviewing themes
5. Defining and naming themes
6. Producing the report

**Best for:** Identifying patterns across datasets

### 2. **Content Analysis**
- Systematic categorization
- Frequency counting
- Deductive or inductive
- Quantify patterns

**Best for:** Structured analysis, comparing datasets

### 3. **Grounded Theory**
- Open coding (initial)
- Axial coding (connections)
- Selective coding (core categories)
- Theory development

**Best for:** Developing new theories from data
